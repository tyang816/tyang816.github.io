# TCM AI Resources

Curated open resources for **Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) LLMs**: papers, open-weight models, benchmarks, datasets, and industry news.

The searchable full catalog (Chinese UI + bilingual metadata) lives at:

**[Browse the TCM catalog → /tcm/]({{ '/tcm/' | relative_url }})**

Data is maintained in [Awesome-TCM-LLM](https://github.com/tyang816/Awesome-TCM-LLM) (`data/catalog.yml` / `catalog.json`) — one source of truth for both the GitHub README and this site.

### What you will find
- Open models and multimodal TCM systems
- Benchmarks and evaluation suites
- Pretraining / SFT datasets and knowledge graphs
- Selected news on TCM AI products and policy

### Contribute
Suggest a resource via [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/tyang816/Awesome-TCM-LLM/issues/new?template=resource.yml).
